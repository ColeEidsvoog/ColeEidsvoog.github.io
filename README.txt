Personal Website
This repository contains the source code for my personal portfolio website, built with CSS, SCSS, JavaScript, and HTML from a boiler plate HTML5 template listed on the bottom of the page. The website consists of three main sections:

About Me
In the About Me section, you can find information about my background, skills, and interests.

Portfolio
The Portfolio section showcases my past projects and experiences. This section is designed to give you an idea of my work and the technologies I have used.

Contact Me
In the Contact Me section, you can find my contact information, including my email address and links to my social media profiles. You can also use the form provided to send me a message directly from my website.
